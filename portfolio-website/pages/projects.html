Projects


For this project I created a concert poster for Lauryn Hill and the 20th anniversary tour for her album, The Miseducation of Lauryn Hill. Her album artwork for The Miseducation of Lauryn Hill, features a photo of the top of a school desk. I wanted to showcase a mix of the old artwork in a new and modern way, as it was the 20th anniversary tour. I decided on a minimalist approach with this design focusing on the nostalgic imagery that is associated with the school desk and the album.
